  • Error-Free Invoicing: Automated mapping ensures that what was shipped matches the invoice exactly, preventing payment delays for the customer.

The ROI for RK Logistics would be realized through massive reductions in "technical debt" and faster "Time-to-Value" for new contracts.

  • Reduced IT Overhead: You eliminate the need for specialized EDI consultants. If your internal IT team can work with RESTful APIs (like Stripe or Twilio), they can manage Mosaic.

Highly Recommended. Standardize EDI across all new sites (Michigan, Arizona) instantly to future-proof your growth. Conduct a pilot integration at one high-volume facility (e.g., Livermore) to replace a legacy EDI connection and measure the reduction in manual error-correction hours.
